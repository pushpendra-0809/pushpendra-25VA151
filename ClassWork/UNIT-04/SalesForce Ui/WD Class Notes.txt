In HTML, (Hyper Text Markup Language)
Semantic Tags define the meaning of the content they contain, while non - semantic tags ony hold content 
and don't indicate its role on the page.
Semantic tags are both human and machine readable, and they can help users and machines understand the 
context and importance of the content .
Non- semantic tags, on the other hand, are used for studying purposes.

HTML ATTRIBUTES
Attributes provide additional information about an elements.
Attributes are also specified for the tags.
